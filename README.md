## 🌿 Southern Marsh Collection — IT Consulting Project

A full-cycle IT consulting engagement for Southern Marsh Collection, a lifestyle apparel brand. This project covers business analysis, system design, project management, and AI-driven digital transformation recommendations — delivered across four milestones with full UML documentation, budget tracking, and issue management.

---

## 📌 Overview

Southern Marsh Collection needed to modernize its digital infrastructure, improve sustainability practices, and drive customer engagement in an increasingly competitive market. Our team analyzed the existing system, identified key gaps, and proposed a phased implementation plan centered on AI-powered tools, a clothing exchange program, and a redesigned digital presence.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Develop a **used clothing exchange and recycling initiative** to improve sustainability
- Implement **AI-powered tools** — virtual try-on, virtual search, digital assistant, and inventory control
- Enhance the **website's SEO** and mobile experience
- Deploy **personalized promotions** and social media campaigns focused on sustainability
- Improve **digital marketing** and customer engagement strategies

## 🧩 System Design

Three UML diagrams were produced to model the proposed system:

- **Use Case Diagram** — models interactions between users and the platform across virtual try-on, exchange system, digital assistant, and inventory management
- **Class Diagram** — defines the core data structures and relationships between entities
- **Sequence Diagram** — illustrates the flow of key processes including virtual try-on requests and exchange transactions
